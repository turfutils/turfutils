Turf EQualized scoring

Unofficial scoring systems for Turfgame.com.

The first one evens out the score by giving the same points for the same work. You get a bonus for taking a lot of zones the last 24 hours.

List two has a max roof of one million points. You get bonus for how many zones you have taken this round and how many of them were unique. You also get a bonus for number of kilometers traveled. Sorry, plane rides do not count. 😁

None of the lists give any points for holding zones. Revisit counts as normal takeover.
